Let's Make Your Newsletter!

Not a consistent texter but still want to stay in touch with your friends post-graduation or post-move? Not a problem with LDRletters!

1. Enter your name and email address.
2. Fill up your newsletter by answering questions (favorite movies this year, music, memories, etc.)
3. Choose a desired newsletter template
4. Compile your emailing friend list
5. Send your newsletter!
